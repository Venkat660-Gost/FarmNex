Rice Blast Disease

About disease:-

Rice blast is one of the most destructive diseases affecting rice crops worldwide, caused by the fungus Magnaporthe oryzae. It affects leaves, stems, nodes, and panicles, causing heavy losses.

Description and Symptoms:

Leaf Blast: Diamond-shaped spots with gray center and dark border. Neck Blast: Causes grain failure. Node Blast: Breaks plant stem. Collar Blast: Kills leaf quickly.

Favorable Conditions:

High humidity, moderate temperature (24°C–28°C), and excess nitrogen increase disease spread.

Management Strategies:

Use resistant varieties, proper water management, and fungicide treatment.
